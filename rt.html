Beautiful Trip to Rjasthan

Rajasthan was also called Rajputana (the country of the Rajputs); It has many tourist attractions and quality tourist facilities. This historical state of India attracts tourists and vacationers with its rich culture, tradition, heritage, and monuments. It has also some sanctuaries and national parks.

Rajasthan is a state in northern India. The state covers an area of 342,239 square kilometres ... To the northeast of these districts is a rugged region (badlands) following the line of the Chambal River.

This is a 5 day trip

Day 1 : Arrival in Jaipur Our tour representative will meet and greet you in Jaipur Airport or Railway Station. He will accompany you to the pre-booked hotel. There he will give you the tour related documents and a short description about the tour. After checking-in relax for the day. Stay overnight at Jaipur.

Day 2 : Jaipur Sightseeing Take an elephant ride in the morning to reach Amber Fort. Later visitsightseeing of City Palace, Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar. In the evening, enjoy Sound and lights show at Amer Fort. Return back to hotel. In the evening go for shopping or dining-out nearby to taste local food. Have a comfortable stay in the hotel at night.

Day 3 : Jaipur - Pushkar (By Road approx 50 Kms / 03 Hrs) Move towards Pushkar in the morning. Pushkar is famous worldwide for organizing the biggest Camel Fair. Check-in to the pre-booked hotel. Later visit the only Brahma Temple. Also take a holy dip in the Pushkar Lake and enjoy the camel ride. Pay a visit to the Rose Garden. Have a comfortable stay at night.

Day 04 : Day 4 : Pushkar - Mount Abu (By train at 0650 / 1140hrs) Day 4 will include the train journey from Pushkar to Mount Abu. Mount Abu - the only hill station of Rajasthan offers a refreshing summer retreat. On reaching Mount Abu, check in to a hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5 : Mount Abu (Departure) The day will begin with the visit to Dilwara Jain Temple in Mount Abu. To carry out the rituals one has to bathe in the lake and avail the facilities from the temple. Enjoy cruising at Nakki Lake. Shop to your heart's content or eat at Mall Road. Move towards Mount Abu Railway Station to board the train for Delhi.
