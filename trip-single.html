Kerala is situated in the south western region of India; Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh are the neighbor states to Kerala. Trivandrum is the capital city of Kerala. Kerala is a tourist destination attracting people by its natural beauty.

This is a 5 day trip

Day 01 : Arrival at Cochin You will get a warm welcome in Traditional Indian style by Travelogy India's executive at Cochin Airport. He will assist you to the hotel and throughout the check in process and will hand over the documents related to tour.

Day 02 : Cochin, Munnar On the second day of the tour, we shall move onwards to Munnar (soon after breakfast). Munnar is the most beautiful Hill Station of South India. Filled with Tea Plantations, it presents greenery at its best. So, on reaching here, the first thing we will do is take you to Tata Tea Factory where you can see the entire process of tea making. After this and South Indian lunch, we shall see Thirumeny Cultural Centre.

Day 03 : Munnar Sightseeing The natural beauty of Munnar is so much that spending even a week in this place seems less. However, we will spend only a few days here. So, on the 2nd day in Munnar; get up to a lazy morning with a hot cup of South Indian Filter Coffee. Enjoy a refreshing breakfast and move on to see more of Munnar.

Day 04 : Alleppey, Kovalam Kovalam which is the next destination is famous for its beaches. It will be afternoon when we will reach here; so we will begin soon after the breakfast.

Day 05 : Kanyakumari,Trivandrum (Departure) Wake up to an early morning and see the Sun Rising at Sun Rise Point. Soon after breakfast, we move on to Trivandrum, your last destination from where you shall catch your flight back. On reaching Trivandrum, we will help you board your flight for further journey.
